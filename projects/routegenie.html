Routegenie

Date: 2025 · Role: Software and Machine Learning

Overview

Slightly different entry on my portfolio compared to my other projects. This was what I made as apart of HackTx 2025. A very big first software project, but I think it went quite well so I wanted to share it here. The product was to make a website that you could use to auto generate routes for the Kilter Board and then send via bluetooth to the board. We used Gemini 2.5- Flash to generate the routes as a json file. I linked our demo video here and our slideshow.
