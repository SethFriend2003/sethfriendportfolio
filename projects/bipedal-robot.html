Bipedal Robot (10× BLDC + ODrive)

Date: 2025 · Role: Controls & Mechatronics

7 kg biped with ten BLDC actuators. Implemented torque control, added external bearing load-path to protect motors, and explored reinforcement learning gait policies.

  • Custom mounts & TPU feet; SendCutSend plates
  • CAN(-FD) motor bus with Teensy 4.1
  • PPO experiments for balance and step cycles
