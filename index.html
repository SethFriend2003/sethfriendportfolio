About

I'm a junior at UT Austin studying electrical and computer engineering, focused on robotics and embedded systems. I like problems that sit at the intersection of math, code, and physical things that move.

Outside of school I've worked on robotic arm deployments at Amazon facilities. I'm currently working on sim-to-real transfer for a 7 kg bipedal humanoid robot — fully assembled in hardware, running a trained RL policy in simulation, working through getting it onto the actual robot.

Before the university projects, a lot of garage builds and broken prototypes. That's still where most of the fun is.
