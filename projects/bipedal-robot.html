Bipedal Robot

Date: 2025 · Role: Controls & Mechatronics

This 7 kg biped robot uses 10 GIM6010-8 BLDC actuators with ODrive-style controllers. I designed custom load-bearing mounts, implemented torque control, and experimented with reinforcement learning gait policies.

  • Designed and fabricated custom aluminum brackets (SendCutSend + 3D printed parts).
  • Implemented CAN-FD bus for motor communication.
  • Trained PPO policies for balance and step cycles.
