Bipedal Robot (10× BLDC + ODrive)

Date: 2025 · Role: Controls & Mechatronics

This project is a 7 kg bipedal robot powered by ten GIM6010-8 BLDC actuators with ODrive-style controllers. My focus was on building a reliable load path, protecting the motors with external bearings, and implementing torque-based control for walking.

Key Work

  • Designed custom aluminum plates and 3D-printed TPU feet for mechanical durability.
  • Implemented CAN-FD motor communication with Teensy 4.1 microcontroller.
  • Developed torque-controlled gait cycle and experimented with reinforcement learning policies.
  • Integrated sensors for stability feedback and motor safety.
